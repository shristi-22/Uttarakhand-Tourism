About Uttarakhand

Discover the Land of Gods - Where Nature Meets Spirituality

The Divine Land

Uttarakhand, often called the "Land of the Gods," is a northern Indian state nestled in the Himalayas. Famed for its breathtaking mountains, sacred rivers, and vibrant culture, Uttarakhand offers an unmatched blend of spirituality, adventure, and natural beauty.

Geography & Climate

Diverse Landscapes

  • 🏔️ Mountains: Home to peaks over 7,000 meters
  • 🌲 Forests: 63% forest cover
  • 🌊 Rivers: Source of major rivers including Ganga and Yamuna
  • 🌡️ Climate Zones: From subtropical to tundra

Cultural Heritage

Traditional Arts & Crafts

  • Aipan (Traditional Floor Art)
  • Ringaal Craft (Bamboo Work)
  • Woolen Handlooms
  • Wooden Crafts

Folk Dance & Music

  • Choliya (Sword Dance)
  • Jhora & Chappeli
  • Bhotiya Folk Songs
  • Traditional Instruments

Local Cuisine

Taste of the Mountains

Uttarakhand's cuisine reflects its diverse geography and cultural heritage. Each region offers unique dishes made from local ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Garhwali Cuisine

  • Kafuli (Spinach Dish)
  • Phaanu (Lentil Dish)
  • Chainsoo
  • Mandua ki Roti

Kumaoni Specialties

  • Bhatt ki Churkani
  • Aloo ke Gutke
  • Bal Mithai
  • Singori
