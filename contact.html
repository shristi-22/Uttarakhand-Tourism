Frequently Asked Questions

Bookings & Reservations

How can I book a tour package?

You can book through our website, email, or by calling our office directly. We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance during peak season.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments. International travelers can pay through secure payment gateways.

Travel Support

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we provide airport transfers from Dehradun and Pantnagar airports. This service needs to be booked in advance.

Can you help with custom itineraries?

Yes, our travel experts can help you create a customized itinerary based on your preferences and duration of stay.
